== Description ==

NewCarNet News will pull in the latest news from the car manufacturer of your choice. Choose one from over 100 manufacturers, or simply get the car latest news.

== Installation ==

1. Download NewCarNet news and unzip
2. Upload newcarnet-news-widget folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
3. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress
4. Add the widget to your sidebar through the "Appearance > Widgets" menu

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How often do your update content? =

 We publish at least 3 news stories a day.

= How far back does your content go? =

 Well over 10 years of content.

= Why are you giving your content away for free? =

 After years of supplying the likes of MSN, Virgin, Tiscali et al with content, we realise that there are a lot of sites out there who would appreciate our content to hang off their site. Enjoy.

= Will this affect my Search Engine Ranking? =

 No. The content is for your site's users only.

= Will this affect NewCarNet's Search Engine Ranking? =

 No. Not until search engines start indexing JavaScript content.

= I don't like the links going off my site. Can I change this? =

 No. Currently we offer the service to help make smaller sites look current and fresh. If you want our full content on your site, you will need to get in touch with our syndication department.
